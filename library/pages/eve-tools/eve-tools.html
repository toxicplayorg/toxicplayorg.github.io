Free tools for EVE Online!

 

This is a list of several EVE tools that are free, and their use does not brake CCP rules.

 

Each article has a brief description of each tool and a link to the download page.

 

Becouse this is a nonprofit website for EVE-Online enthusiasts we strive to find only free and from CCP allowed tools that improve and enrich the EVE-Online gaming experience.

o7

KNOWN SPACE COMBAT SITES!

Combat sites are a broad class of locations involving player versus environment (PvE) combat, usually in engagements against pirate faction enemies.

Combat sites are usually found via exploration and most fall into one of two broad categories: combat anomalies and cosmic signatures.

Link To UNIWIKI

WORMHOLE SPACE COMBAT SITES!

Wormhole Space, also known as the Anoikis Galaxy, is a collection of semi-charted systems full of opportunity and danger.

Wormhole Space has roughly 2600 known systems, each classified as one of six levels. These are commonly referred to as C1, C2, C3, C4, C5 or C6

Link To UNIWIKI

Check before you jump tool!

The 'basic' gatecamp checker will always be 100% free of charge. If, however, you want to gain even more of an advantage over your enemies, consider purchasing some Premium Credit.

To check out the benefits, log in with your EVE character, using the button at the top-right of your screen.

Link To Gatecamp Check

PYFA (Python Fitting Assistant)!

PYFA (Python Fitting Assistant) is a cross-platform fitting application that can be used natively on any platform where python and wxwidgets are available, including Windows, Mac and Linux.

It provides many advanced features such as graphs and full calculations of any possible combination of modules, fits, etc.

Link To PYFA

Evepraisal is tool for price check!

Find something in Eve Online that you want to price check like a contract, cargo scan result, etc. Press CTRL + A, CTRL + C, then click on the lovely box to the left or above this and press CTRL + V.

Submit and you should see a price breakdown of all the items.

Link To Evepraisal

Eve Marketer - Market tool !

Eve Market data is lacking critical features. Such as accessing market data in a different region, or doing market analysis universe wide, or loading market data from outside the game.

That's where EVE Marketer comes in. We collect data from ALL regions and provide it to you.

Link To EVE Marketer

EVE-ONLINE killboard tool!

Killboard is a statistical tool of destroyed ships and capsules. With mentioned statistics the average player can get various information about other players' habits, potentially dangerous systems, equipment of individual ships, etc.

A tool that makes traveling and living in all of New-Eden easier.

Link To Zkillboard

Planning for Planetary Interaction

Planetary Interaction is a type of Industry that allows pilots to create industrial colonies on just about any planet in the EVE universe.

To interact with planet, pilot need to purchase the correct Command Center for their chosen planet from the market.

Link To PI planning

The greatest wormhole mapper ever!

Tripwire is an open source wormhole mapping tool, hosted for free to the public, built for use with EVE Online. Using the latest in internet security standards it is the most secure tool in New Eden.

A tool that makes traveling and living in a wormhole easier.

Link To Tripwire

EVE-ONLINE known space mapper!

The maps are focused to be clean and easily readable with the most important information for fleet roaming and planning.

They contain daily sovereignty and outpost information for your favourite 0.0 regions. Occupancy information for factional warfare is also included.

Link To DOTLAN

EVE-ONLINE "WHO" info. tool!

Different information can be found here, such as, the number of members of a particular corporation, when a particular character tend to be more active, in what compositions of the fleet player prefers to fly (if the character is present on the Zkillboard), in which region he or she is he present most etc.

Link To Eve-Who

EVE Online Missions!

A mission in EVE Online is a job offered by an non-playing character to a player which requires the player to accomplish a set of objectives in exchange for a set of rewards.

Helpful guides with a detailed descriptions on how to run all the various missions on EVE Online and more!

Link To Missions
